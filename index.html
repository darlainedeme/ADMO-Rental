A note from me

I wanted you to feel at home from day one.

I moved to Adams Morgan (AdMo, for friends) because I wanted somewhere that actually felt alive, and it absolutely delivered. I need to leave DC momentarily and didn't want to hand the place over to a stranger without a proper introduction. So, because I'm a bit of a nerd and I love maps and charts, I built this. Every spot I'd recommend to a friend is on it, with my favorites starred so you have a handful of go-tos for the first week. After that, please go explore on your own. If you find something good I missed, tell me when I'm back.
